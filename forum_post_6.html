How to Learn Braille?
6 days ago ¬∑ User Joannnne
Learning Braille was something my family and I explored after my diagnosis. My parents managed to find a local community class that offered Braille literacy lessons specifically designed for beginners.

The instructors are qualified and experienced, and they use large embossed books along with tactile games to help with memorization. The course included both Grade 1 and Grade 2 Braille, and it was really well structured.

I also practiced a lot at home with simple tools like a Braille slate and stylus, and later got a Braille typewriter. There are also mobile apps that help with recognition training.
