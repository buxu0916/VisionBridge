How Can I Get Bantuan OKU?
Posted by Nasi_Kandar ¬∑ 2m ago
Photostat your IC and bring along the OKU cert with you.For your information,

1. Prepare a copy of your MyKad (IC) and the official OKU card from JKM (Jabatan Kebajikan Masyarakat).
2. Go to the nearest Social Welfare Office (Pejabat Kebajikan Masyarakat) and fill in the bantuan form.
3. You may also need to bring a passport-sized photo and supporting medical documents if required.

Some offices need an appointment. The staff were quite helpful at Kepong branch.
